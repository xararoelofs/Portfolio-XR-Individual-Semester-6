My name is Xara.

I am a media student who is deeply interested in media psychology, movies, content, writing and communication. My latest addiction is listening to podcasts about current news and topics, conversations from friends / experts and interviews.

Within media, the things that interest me most are how people can be influenced by certain media, how it shapes the way people think and perceive and how to respond or react to that. Certain media can be texts, movies, photos, campaigns and so much more.

Others (and I), would describe me as devoted, hardworking, initiating and social. Of course, these are just words, a fragment of me. If you are really interested in getting to know me, you'd have to get to know me.
